# Family Entity

**Document ID:** LOS-DAT-ENT-011

**Status:** Draft

**Owner:** Data Team

---

# Purpose

The Family Entity represents family relationships, activities, events, and shared responsibilities managed by Life OS.

---

# Responsibilities

- Store family records.
- Track family activities.
- Track shared responsibilities.
- Support family reporting.
- Support family analytics.

---

- Produces Family Reports
- Produces Family Statistics

- Relationship required.
